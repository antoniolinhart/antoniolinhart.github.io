Overview

Hey! My name is Antonio Linhart. I am originally from Overland Park, Kansas. I am a sophomore at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln in the Raikes School of Computer Science and Management. I've been interested in technology from a very young age, originally because of video games, but now I am interested in developing technology to make my and other people's lives easier. Currently, I am trying to work on some personal web development projects like this website, since I am not extremely familiar with user interfaces and web design!

Outside of academics, I am a Teaching Assistant for introductory Computer Science classes, the secretary of the Mexican American Student Association, a chair for on-campus Honors recruitment within the Honors Ambassadors, and a mentor for the Computer Science and Engineering program. I am also a fan of Huskers football!
